New to Climbing?

You don’t need to have any experience to get started and we have climbs for all skill levels.

Bouldering is one of the main disciplines of climbing. You don’t need a rope, harness, or belayer—the walls aren’t very tall and are built over a padded floor. Free from the need of specialized knowledge or equipment, bouldering is a beautifully simple, social, and fun way to climb.
